How to Play WordRang

Welcome to WordRang! Here's how to play the game and enjoy its features:

Starting the Game

  • Enter Your Name: Begin by entering your name in the provided text box on the welcome screen. Click the "Start Game" button to begin.
  • Start Game Without Name: If you try to start the game without entering your name, a popup will remind you to enter it before proceeding.

Playing the Game

  • Scramble a Word: Once the game starts, click "Next Word" to generate a scrambled word from the dictionary.
  • Guess the Word: Type your guess and click "Submit Guess" to check if your guess is correct.
  • Receiving Feedback: The game will tell you whether your guess was correct or incorrect.
  • Moving to the Next Word: Click "Next Word" to proceed to the next scrambled word and continue the game.

Voice Assistance

Click on the speaker icon next to the scrambled word to hear the word pronounced.

Scoring and Progress

  • Score Tracking: Your current score is displayed at the bottom of the game screen.
  • Word Count: The game also tracks how many words you've played.

Exiting the Game

Click the "Exit Game" button to exit and reset the game.

Hint: Word Starts with Capital Letter
